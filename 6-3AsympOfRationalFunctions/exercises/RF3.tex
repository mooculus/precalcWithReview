\begin{exercise}

  Rewrite the rational function $$  f(x) = \frac{x^2-4}{x-1} + \frac{x^2-3x}{x-2}  $$in the form $f(x) = p(x)/q(x)$.

  Answer: $f(x) = \frac{\answer{2x^3-x-6x^2+8}}{\answer{x^2-3x+2}}$.
  
\end{exercise}
