\begin{exercise}
Use factoring to solve the equation.\\
$x^4-81=0$\\
$(\answer{x^2}-\answer{9})(\answer{x^2}+\answer{9})=0$\\
$(\answer{x^2}+\answer{9})(x+\answer{3})(x-\answer{3})=0$\\
Largest value of $x=\answer{3}$\\
Least value of $x=\answer{-3}$\\
\end{exercise}
