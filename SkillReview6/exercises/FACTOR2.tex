\begin{exercise}
Use factoring to solve the equation.\\
$3x^3+13x^2+12x+7=7$\\
$x(\answer{3x^2+13x+12})=0$\\
$x(3x+\answer{4})(x+\answer{3})=0$\\
Largest value of $x=\answer{0}$\\
Middle value of $x=\answer{-\frac{4}{3}}$\\
Least value of $x=\answer{-3}$\\
\end{exercise}
