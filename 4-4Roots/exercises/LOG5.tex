\begin{exercise}
Evaluate the following logarithmic expressions.\\
$ \log_b{b^{\frac{2}{3}}}=\answer{\frac{2}{3}}$ \\
$ \log_{b^2}{b^{\frac{2}{3}}}=\answer{\frac{1}{3}}$ \\
$ \log_{b^{\frac{1}{3}}}{b^{\frac{2}{3}}}=\answer{2}$ 
\end{exercise}
