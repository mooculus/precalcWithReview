\begin{exercise}
Solve the equation for $x$.\\
$\frac{2}{3}x+8 = -4$\\

Complete this step in the solution process.
$x=\answer{\frac{3}{2}}(-4-8)$

Now give the final answer.
$x=\answer{-18}$
\end{exercise}
