\begin{exercise}
Let $f$ be a circular function with amplitude 5, midline $2$, and period $\frac{1}{2}$ such that $f(3) = 7$.

A formula for $f$ in terms of the cosine function is $f(x) = \answer{5}\cos\left(\answer{4\pi}(x - 3)\right) + \answer{2}$. 
\end{exercise}
