\begin{exercise}
Let $f$ be a circular function defined by $f(x) = 7 \cos\left(3\pi(x - 2)\right) - 12$.

The midline of $f$ is $\answer{-12}$. The amplitude of $f$ is $\answer{7}$. The period of $f$ is $\answer{2/3}$. The range of $f$ is $[\answer{-19}, \answer{-5}]$.
\end{exercise}
