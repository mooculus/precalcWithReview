How many solutions does the following equation have? \\
\[
{(\sqrt{x})}^2=x
\]

\choice{Exactly $1$}  
\choice{$2$}  
\choice{All Real Numbers}
\choice[correct]{All Non-Negative Real Numbers}  
\choice{None}
\end{multipleChoice}
