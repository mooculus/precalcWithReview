\begin{exercise}
The position of a car at time $t$ (in hours) is given by $s(t) = 8t^2 -3t+4$ (in miles). 

The average rate of change of $s$ over the interval $[0,4]$ represents:
\begin{multipleChoice}
\choice{the average position of the car over the interval.}
\choice[correct]{the average velocity of the car over the interval.}
\choice{the average acceleration of the car over the interval.}
\choice{the average distance covered by the car.}
\end{multipleChoice}
\begin{exercise}
What is the average speed of the car over the time interval $[0,4]$?\\
$\answer{29}$ miles per hour.
\end{exercise}

\end{exercise}
