\begin{exercise}
Find the zeros of the following function.
\[
z(x)=7x(3x-2)(x+8)(x-\sqrt{7})(x+\sqrt{7})
\]
Enter the $x$ values from smallest to largest:

$x_1 = \answer{-8}$ 

$x_2  = \answer{-\sqrt{7}}$

$x_3 = \answer{0}$ 

$x_4  = \answer{\frac{2}{3}}$ 

$x_5 = \answer{\sqrt{7}}$


\end{exercise}
