\begin{exercise}

  Rewrite the rational function \[  f(x) = \frac{x^3+4x}{x^2+2} - \frac{x+3}{x^2-1}  \]in the form $f(x) = p(x)/q(x)$.

  Answer: $f(x) = \frac{\answer{x^5+2x^3-3x^2-6x-6}}{\answer{x^4-x^2+2x^2-2}}$.
\end{exercise}
