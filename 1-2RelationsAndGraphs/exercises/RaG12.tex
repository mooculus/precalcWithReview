\begin{exercise}

The graph below is the map of a city.

\begin{image}
\begin{tikzpicture}
    \begin{axis}[xlabel={},ylabel={},
                xmin=-9,xmax=1,
                ymin=-9,ymax=1,
                xtick={-8,-6,...,8},
                ytick={-8,-6,...,8},
                clip=false]
        \addplot[soliddot] coordinates {(-5,-4)} node[below]{\large A};
        \addplot[soliddot] coordinates {(0, -2)} node[above]{\large B};
        \addplot[soliddot] coordinates {(-2, -4)} node[right]{\large C};
        \addplot[soliddot] coordinates {(-7, 0)} node[above]{\large D};
        \addplot[soliddot] coordinates {(-4, -4)} node[below]{\large E};
        \addplot[soliddot] coordinates {(-8, -5)} node[below]{\large F};
	\addplot[soliddot] coordinates {(-1, -8)} node[below]{\large G};	
    \end{axis}
\end{tikzpicture}
\end{image}

Given the Cartesian coordinates of a location, determine the corresponding point on the graph.

The basketball court is at $(-8, -5).  \answer{F}$\\
The art studio is at $(-5,-4).  \answer{A}$\\
The tatoo parlor is at $(-4,-4).  \answer{E}$\\
The day care is at $(-1,-8).  \answer{G}$\\
The auto shop is at $(-7,0).  \answer{D}$\\
The cellphone store is at $(0,-2).  \answer{B}$\\
The mini-mart is at $(-2,-4).  \answer{C}$
