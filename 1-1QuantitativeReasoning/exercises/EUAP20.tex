A bath tub is 6000 cubic inches. How big is the bath tub in cubic feet?\\ 
$6000$ cubic inches = $\frac{6000}{\answer{1728}}$\\
$6000$ cubic inches = $\answer{3.47}$ cubic feet \calcHW (Round to two decimal places)
