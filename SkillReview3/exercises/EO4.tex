\begin{exercise}
Evaluate $f(a+1)$ and $f(-a-1)$.\\
$f(x)= x^3+x$\\
$f(a+1)=\answer{a^3+3a^2+4a+2}$\\
$f(-a-1)=\answer{-(a^3+3a^2+4a+2)}$
\end{exercise}
