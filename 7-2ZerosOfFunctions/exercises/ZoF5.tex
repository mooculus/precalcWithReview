\begin{exercise}
The equation $12x - 3 = -5 - x$ can be rewritten as $f(x) = 0$ for some function $f$.
In this case,
$$
f(x) = \answer{13}x + 2.
$$

The zero of $f$ is $\answer{-\frac{2}{13}}$.

\end{exercise}
