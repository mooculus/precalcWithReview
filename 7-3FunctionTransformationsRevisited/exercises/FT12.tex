\begin{exercise}

Find the equation of the reflection of $f$ across the $x$ axis and the $y$ axis.\\

$f(x)=2\sqrt{x+3}-4$

Reflection across the x-axis: $g(x)=\answer{-2\sqrt{x+3}+4}$\\
Reflection across the y-axis: $g(x)=\answer{2\sqrt{3-x}-4}$\\




\end{exercise}
